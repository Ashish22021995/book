Shunyam Sāmyasamuchchaye enables quick identification of hidden symmetry in algebraic and rational expressions by equating common terms (samuccaya) to zero. This powerful shortcut is especially effective in scientific analysis, where it helps simplify equilibrium equations, ratios, and concentrations, saving time and enhancing pattern recognition in complex chemical relationships.
