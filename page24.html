Example 1: Solve the system of linear equations

45x - 23y = 113 ... ...(i)

23x - 45y = 91 ... ...(ii)

Step 1: Add the equations

Adding equations (i) and (ii):

(45x-23y)+(23x-45y)=113+91

(45x+23x)+(-23y-45y)= 204

68x-68y= 204

x - y =
204
68
= 3 ... ...(iii)

Step 2: Subtract the equations

Subtract equation (ii) from equation (i):

(45x-23y)-(23x-45y)=113-91

(45x-23x)+(-23y+45y)= 22

22x+22y= 22

x + y =
22
22
= 1 ... ...(iv)

Step 3: Solve the simplified equations

From equations (iii) and (iv):

x - y = 3

x + y = 1

Adding these equations:

2x = 4 ⇒ x = 2

Subtracting these equations:

2y = -2 ⇒ y = -1

Solution:

x = 2, y = -1
