APPLICATIONS OF SUB-SUTRAS OF VEDIC MATHEMATICS

The sub-sutras of Vedic Mathematics serve as a valuable asset for higher secondary school students, especially those studying science. These concise and intuitive mathematical principles simplify calculations that frequently appear in subjects such as Chemistry, Physics and advanced Mathematics. Many scientific problems demand quick and accurate arithmetic solutions for a problem. Such as squaring numbers, solving algebraic expressions, or working with proportions and this is where sub-sutras like Yāvadūnam Tāvadūnam (reduce by the deficiency and add its square) or Antyayor-Dasake’pi (useful in completing base-10 squares and cubes) become extremely effective. These techniques reduce cognitive load during complex calculations, allowing students to focus more on concepts rather than lengthy computations.
