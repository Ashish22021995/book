Problem: Calculate the total number of atoms in 154 grams of oxygen gas (O2).

Given Data:

The molecular weight of O2: 32 g/mol.

Avogadro’s number: 6.022×1023 molecules/mol.

To calculate the total number of atoms, we first find the number of moles of O2 and multiply by Avogadro’s number, then account for the fact that each molecule of O2 contains 2 atoms.
