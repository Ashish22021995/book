Steps to Use Guṇitasamuccaya Sūtra:

1. Identify the factors: Break the given polynomial or expression into its factors.

2. Sum the coefficients of each factor: Calculate the sum of coefficients for each factor (ignore the variable terms and add the coefficients as numbers).

3. Multiply the sums of coefficients: Find the product of the sums of coefficients.

4. Sum coefficients of the original polynomial: Do the same for the expanded (or original) polynomial.

5. Verify: Compare the two results. If they match, the factorization or computation is correct.

Example 1: Factorization Verification

Verify: (y + 2) (y + 3) (y + 5)

Step 1: Sum coefficients of each factor

For (y + 2), the coefficients are 1 (of y) and 2 : Sum = 1 + 2 = 3

For (y + 3), the coefficients are 1 and 3 : Sum = 1 + 3 = 4

For (y + 5), the coefficients are 1 and 5 : Sum = 1 + 5 = 6

Step 2: Multiply sums of coefficients

Product of sums = 3 × 4 × 6 = 72

Step 3: Expand the product

Expand (y + 2) (y + 3) (y + 5) = (y + 2) ((y + 3) (y + 5))

= (y + 2) (y 2 + 8y + 15)

= y 3 + 8y 2 + 15y + 2y 2 + 16y + 30

= y 3 + 10y 2 + 31y + 30

Step 4: Sum coefficients of the expanded polynomial

1 + 10 + 31 + 30 = 72

Step 5: Verify

Product of sums (72) = Sum of coefficients of expanded polynomial (72)

Conclusion: The factorization is correct.
