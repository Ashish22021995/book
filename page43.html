Its relevance extends to scientific domains as well. In physical chemistry, where mole fractions and concentration ratios frequently yield recurring decimal values, this sutra aids in obtaining exact representations quickly and reliably. Ultimately, Śeṣānyankena Carameṇa reinforces the elegance of mathematical cycles, transforming what appears to be a complex decimal expansion into an intuitive pattern which simply revealed through remainders and repetition.
