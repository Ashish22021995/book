Example 2: Verification in Division

Solve: Divide y 3 + 6y 2 + 11y + 6 by (y + 1).

Step 1: Factorize the polynomial

Perform synthetic or polynomial division:

y 3 + 6y 2 + 11y + 6 ÷ (y + 1) = (y + 1) (y 2 + 5y + 6)

Step 2: Sum coefficients of each factor

For (y + 1), the sum is : 1 + 1 = 2

For (y 2 + 5y + 6), the sum is : 1 + 5 + 6 = 12

Step 3: Multiply sums of coefficients

Product of sums = 2 × 12 = 24

Step 4: Sum coefficients of the original polynomial

The sum of coefficients of y 3 + 6y 2 + 11y + 6 is: 1 + 6 + 11 + 6 = 24

Step 5: Verify

Product of sums (24) = Sum of coefficients of the polynomial (24)

Conclusion: The division is correct.
