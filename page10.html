Example 2: Multiply 34 × 56

1. Write the numbers: 34 and 56.

2. Steps:

  • Multiply the unit digits: 4 × 6 = 24. Write 4 in the units place and carry over 2.

  • Multiply crosswise: (3 × 6) + (4 × 5) = 18 + 20 = 38. Add the carry-over 2: 38 + 2 = 40.
    Write 0 in the tens place and carry over 4.

  • Multiply the tens digits: 3 × 5 = 15. Add the carry-over 4: 15 + 4 = 1915 + 4 = 19.

3. Result Combine the digits: 1904.
