Example 3: Multiplication by 12

Multiply 824 by 12.

Step 1: Write the number with a zero before and after:

824 → 08240

Step 2: Apply the sutra step-by-step:

First step: Last digit 0 (ultimate) and second-last digit 4 (penultimate):

0 + 2(4) = 0 + 8 = 8

Write 8, no carryover.

Second step: 4 (ultimate) and 2 (penultimate):

4 + 2(2) = 4 + 4 = 8

Write 8, no carryover.

Third step: 2 (ultimate) and 8 (penultimate):

2 + 2(8) = 2 + 16 = 18

Write 8, carry over 1.

Fourth step: 8 (ultimate) and 0 (penultimate):

8 + 2(0) = 8

Add the carryover 1: 8 + 1 = 9.

Step 3: Write the final result:

824 × 12 = 9888

Answer : 824 × 12 = 9888.
