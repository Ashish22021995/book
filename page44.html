The Sesanyankena Caramena sutra offers a clever and efficient method to convert fractions into recurring decimal form by using remainders and their relationship with the last digit of the denominator. Especially valuable in chemistry for mole fractions and precise ratio expressions, this sutra transforms repetitive division into a simple, structured pattern, making decimal expansion more intuitive and accessible.
