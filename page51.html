Application of Ekanyunena Purvena in chemistry: Moles of Gas

Problem: A reaction produces 99 moles of a substance, and each mole of the substance contains 44 g. Calculate the total mass of the substance used.

Solution:

To find the total mass, we compute

Total Mass=Moles×Molar mass

Total Mass=99×44

Step 1: Identify the Structure

The multiplier is 99 (repetitive digits), which matches the sutra’s condition. The multiplicand is 44 (the same number of digits).

Step 2: Subtract 1 from the Multiplicant

44 − 1 = 43 (Left-hand side of the product)
